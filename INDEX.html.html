THE SPA AT THE REVERIE SAIGON

COME TO OUR SPA, MORE THAN AN EXPERIENCE, YOU WILL GET MORE….


The Spa at The Reverie Saigon, together with the fitness centre, encompasses 1,200 square metres over two floors.

With 12 private treatment rooms, separate men’s & women’s steam and sauna facilities, a beauty salon as well as a hair salon, the city’s most luxurious spa presents an oasis of calm and rejuvenation in the heart of bustling Ho Chi Minh City.

Its comprehensive menu is centred on holistic therapies that draw from the depths of Southeast Asian spa traditions as well as Western-inspired aromatherapy treatments to relax and rejuvenate mind, body and soul.
